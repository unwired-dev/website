# Unwired Mail

Unwired Mail is a coming soon email app for macOS, iOS, and iPadOS focused on personal communication, privacy-first defaults, and on-device AI.

## Why it exists

Email carries personal context: conversations, decisions, reminders, receipts, and relationships. Unwired Mail is being designed around the idea that useful AI for email should work close to that context without turning the inbox into another cloud data source.

The product is also proof of Unwired's product standards. The same attention to interface quality, privacy-aware decisions, and practical AI behavior behind Unwired Mail informs Unwired's Frontend Consultancy work for product teams.

## What it is being designed to do

- Keep personal email workflows focused on reading, triage, and follow-up.
- Use on-device AI where local context can improve daily email decisions.
- Treat Apple platforms as the first-class product surface.
- Make privacy a product constraint, not an afterthought.

## Local AI and privacy decisions

Unwired Mail's AI direction starts from a simple constraint: personal mail context should stay close to the person using it. On-device AI is the product choice for features that need sensitive inbox context, so the app can help with personal communication without defaulting to cloud model processing for that context.

That constraint shapes the product more than a feature checklist. It affects what gets built, what stays manual, and how the interface explains AI-assisted behavior.
