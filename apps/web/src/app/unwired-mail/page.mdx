# Unwired Mail

Unwired Mail is Unwired's product proof on the company site: a privacy-focused email client for macOS and iOS with local AI.

## Why it belongs here

For the company website, Unwired Mail is not the primary conversion path. Its role is to demonstrate product judgment, execution quality, and a bias toward trustworthy software behavior.

## Product posture

- Privacy-first by design
- Local AI as a product choice, not a data-harvesting strategy
- Built for Apple platforms
- Positioned as evidence of the standards behind Unwired's consultancy work
