# Unwired Calendar

Unwired Calendar is a coming soon calendar app for macOS, iOS, and iPadOS centered on on-device AI for remembering what matters.

## Why it exists

Calendars carry personal context: commitments, focus blocks, travel, relationships, and the small details that make time useful. Unwired Calendar is being designed around the idea that useful AI for time should work close to that context without turning a personal schedule into another cloud data source.

The product shares Unwired's broader product philosophy with Unwired Mail: personal communication and time should be helped by AI that respects where personal context belongs.

## What it is being designed to do

- Keep calendar workflows focused on commitments, context, and follow-through.
- Use on-device AI where local schedule context can improve daily planning decisions.
- Treat Apple platforms as the first-class product surface.
- Make remembering what matters feel practical instead of noisy.

## Local AI and time context

Unwired Calendar's AI direction starts from a simple constraint: personal time context should stay close to the person using it. On-device AI is the product choice for features that need sensitive calendar context, so the app can help with personal planning without defaulting to cloud model processing for that context.

That constraint shapes the product more than a feature checklist. It affects what gets built, what stays manual, and how the interface explains AI-assisted behavior.
